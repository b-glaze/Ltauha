Welcome to Ltauha!

We are a great restaurant located in Ridgewood, Queens NY! Our menu is classic American fare. Whether you're in the mood for one of the best cheeseburgers in Queens or a steak cooked to perfection, we have you covered. Lunch and dinner are served during the week with a delicious brunch menu added during the weekend. With a relaxed atmoshpere and an attentive, friendly staff you're sure to have a great time.

Do you have a special occasion coming up? We have a beautiful room for private parties. Party menus can be prix fixe or a la carte, whichever suits you best.

Take a look at some of our menu items in the gallery below. After you browse our full menu you're sure to be hungry!
